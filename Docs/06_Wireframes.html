Graphxy Labs · Wireframes · v0.3 · Structural Layout Only

Wireframe documentation — layout, hierarchy, content zones

Structural wireframes only. No colors, no real typography, no visual styling. Every region is labeled with its content type and structural role. Four surface areas: Graphxy Labs landing page, Graphzy product app, and the Clampbox, Forkline, & Lattice detail pages. All screens are light-mode. Product previews utilize a unified interactive motion system (rather than static designs), which is shared directly across the landing page lists and individual detail pages for consistency.
